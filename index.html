1. Players: Two players (X and O) take turns.

2. Objective: Get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

3. GamePlay: Players take turns placing their marks in empty squares.

4. Winning: The first player to align three marks wins.

5. Draw: If all squares are filled and no player wins, the game is a draw.

6. Reset: Players can reset the board and scores to start a new game.
